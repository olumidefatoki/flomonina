About Us

About The Company

FloMuvina Nigeria Limited (FloMuvina) is a supply chain ag-startup focused on building a resilient ag-commodity supply chain by creating a system that allows critical ecosystem actors to interact and transact in trust and transparency while catalyzing sustainable supply of ag-commodity to local processors and international market off-takers through grain aggregation, warehousing, and semi-last mile supply at scale.

FloMuvina commenced operations in full in the thick of the COVID-19 pandemic and has to date supplied more than 50,000MT of grains (maize, paddy rice and soybean) in 2021 to date. FloMuvina leverages the power of technology, supply chain expertise and a strong ecosystem social capital to deliver unique value to local processors who seek to establish a consistent and elastic supply system for the agro-commodities required to drive their processing operations.
