Who we are

FloMuvina is an integrated agricultural supply chain catalyst ensuring farmers’ produce are off-taken at scale in the right quality and quantity to meet local processing and international market needs.

Our Vision
We are building an agricultural supply chain system that will become the largest, most reliable, and most resilient in the country and across the continent.

Our Mission
To do this, we are bringing together very highly skilled young minds, the best and most suitable technology, and the strongest ecosystem relationships to ensure agro-commodities are always available to food processors

Our Unique Story

Our Timeline

October 2019 -FloMuvina Nig Ltd was registered in Nigeria as a legal entity

FloMuvina Nig Ltd was registered in Nigeria as a legal entity.

February 2020 -FloMuvina launched as a super aggregator

FloMuvina launched as a super aggregator supplying agro-commodities (maize, rice paddy and soybean) to food processors in Nigeria through a 3rd party.

December 2020 -FloMuvina partners with ThriveAgric.

FloMuvina partners with ThriveAgric to aggregate approximately 50,000MT of maize, rice paddy and soybean

April 2021 -FloMuvina expands its partnership with ThriveAgric

FloMuvina expands its partnership with ThriveAgric to supply food processors directly with paddy rice.

June 2021 -FloMuvina increased its warehousing capacity

FloMuvina increased its warehousing capacity from 1,100MT to 5,700MT.

September 2021 -FloMuvina raises $1.6m for trade financing

FloMuvina raises about N637m ($1.6m) for trade financing.
